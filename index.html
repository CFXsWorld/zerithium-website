How Zerithium Protocol Works

Zerithium is a decentralized non-custodial liquidity protocol where users can participate as depositors or borrowers.

1

Deposit Assets

Supply assets to the protocol's liquidity pools and earn passive income through interest generated by borrowers.

2

Earn Interest

Receive zTokens that represent your share of the liquidity pool and accrue interest in real-time.

3

Borrow Assets

Use your deposited assets as collateral to borrow other assets with either variable or stable interest rates.

4

Manage Risk

Monitor your health factor to ensure your collateral remains sufficient or risk liquidation.
